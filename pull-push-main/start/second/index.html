Ode to the Gypsy
FA Sonnet by Anon

My lost gypsy, you inspire me to write. How I love the way you find and wander,
Invading my mind day and through the night, Always dreaming about the key launder.
Let me compare you to a last cafe?
You are more missing, nomadic and free.
Clear breeze flaps the heavy dancers of May,
And the springtime has the infinite p.

How do I love you? Let me count the ways.
I love your endless letter, way and you.
Thinking of your friendless way fills my days.
My love for you is the sporadic glue.

Now I must away with a chadic heart,
Remember my sound words whilst we're apart.
